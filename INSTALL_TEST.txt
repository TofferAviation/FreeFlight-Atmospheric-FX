FFATMOSPHERICS v0.4 - INSTALL AND FIRST WAKE TEST
============================================

1. Finish the Windows build in BUILD_WINDOWS.txt.

2. Copy the entire folder:
     package\FFAtmo
   into:
     X-Plane 12\Resources\plugins\

3. Confirm this exact installed structure:
     Resources\plugins\FFAtmo\64\win.xpl
     Resources\plugins\FFAtmo\assets\FFAtmo_Lineage.obj
     Resources\plugins\FFAtmo\assets\FFAtmo_particles.pss
     Resources\plugins\FFAtmo\assets\FFAtmo_particles.png
     Resources\plugins\FFAtmo\profiles\Lineage1000.ini

4. Start X-Plane 12 and load the X-Crafts Lineage 1000.

5. Open Log.txt and confirm:
     [FFAtmo] Started.
     [FFAtmo] Loaded aircraft profile: X-Crafts Lineage 1000

6. Before chasing real weather, use:
     Plugins > FFAtmospherics > Preview Mode
   This forces both contrails, over-wing condensation and every v0.4 wake stage
   live. The preview does not alter X-Plane weather.

7. Fly straight and level at cruise speed for at least three minutes. From an
   external rear-quarter or tower view, look for this sequence:

   - first seconds: thin, bright twin engine trails;
   - young wake: two plumes roll into opposite corkscrews and
     begin descending below the original flight level;
   - middle wake: a fainter ice curtain remains between the descending wake and
     the original trail altitude;
   - older wake: the paired structure becomes broader and irregular, then
     spreads into wispy contrail cirrus.

8. Inspect emitter alignment. Record whether each
   engine stream begins forward/aft/high/low of the nozzle and whether the two
   wingtip streams begin at the actual tips. Also record whether the primary
   wake visibly rotates in opposite directions and descends without dropping
   unrealistically fast.

9. Disable Preview Mode. For a natural persistence test, use cold cruise air
   near FL350 with a dew point close to ambient temperature. Suitable conditions
   should produce roughly 180-300 seconds (about 45-75 km at cruise speed).

10. Settings are saved automatically to:
     X-Plane 12\Output\preferences\FFAtmo.ini
